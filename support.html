Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple or Google, not by NoteSurfing directly.

  • iOS: Open Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions → NoteSurfing and tap Cancel Subscription.
  • Android: Open the Google Play Store → Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → NoteSurfing and tap Cancel.

Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period.

How do I restore a previous purchase?

Open the app, go to the paywall (any locked level or Settings → Upgrade to PRO), and tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID or Google account used for the original purchase.

The microphone isn't detecting my voice

  • Verify NoteSurfing has microphone permission in your device settings.
  • Make sure no other app (calls, Zoom, voice memos) is using the microphone in the background.
  • Try in a quiet room — background noise can interfere with note detection.
  • If it still fails, restart the app and try again.

How do I change my vocal range (tesitura)?

Go to Settings → Vocal range and re-run the detection. The app will transpose intonation exercises to match your new range.

I forgot my password

On the login screen, tap Forgot password? and enter your email. You'll receive a reset link within a few minutes (check your spam folder if it doesn't arrive). If you signed up with Google or Apple, use that same provider — there's no password to reset.

How do I report a bug or request a feature?

Email us at support@notesurfing.com with:

  • Your device model and OS version
  • The app version (visible in Settings at the bottom)
  • Steps to reproduce the bug, or a screen recording if possible

Refunds

Refunds are handled by Apple or Google according to their respective policies. Request them directly at reportaproblem.apple.com (iOS) or through your Google Play purchase history (Android).
