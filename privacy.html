Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 21, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 21, 2026

NoteSurfing ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information when you use the NoteSurfing mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

Account information. When you register using Google or Apple Sign-In, we receive your name and email address from the identity provider. We do not receive or store your passwords.

Usage and progress data. We collect information about your activity within the App, including lessons completed, streaks, scores, rankings, and subscription status. This data is stored in our database (Supabase) to sync your progress across devices.

Audio recordings. Both the reading and intonation modules use your device's microphone.

  • Reading module: short audio clips (typically 5–10 seconds) are transmitted to OpenAI's speech recognition service for real-time transcription. We do not retain the original audio on our servers. OpenAI may retain it for up to 30 days under its API data usage policy for abuse prevention, after which it is deleted automatically.
  • Intonation module: audio is processed entirely on your device using local pitch-detection algorithms. Audio from this module does not leave your device.

Crash and performance data. We use Sentry to collect anonymous crash reports and performance metrics (e.g. device model, OS version, stack traces when the App fails). This data helps us diagnose and fix issues.

Payment information. Purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple App Store, Google Play, and RevenueCat. We do not collect or store your payment card details. We receive only confirmation of purchase status from these providers.

Device information. We may collect basic device information such as operating system version and device type to ensure compatibility and improve performance.

Note on audio data and model training: We are developing our own recognition models with the long-term goal of providing real-time feedback entirely on-device, without relying on external services. During this development phase, transcription results and analytics derived from your reading-module audio may be used in aggregated form to train and improve those models. Original audio recordings are not retained on our servers. You may request deletion of derived data at any time by contacting us.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and personalize the App experience
  • To sync your progress and streaks across devices
  • To process and verify your subscription status
  • To improve pitch and reading recognition accuracy through model training on aggregated, non-retained audio
  • To diagnose crashes and performance issues
  • To communicate with you about your account or the App
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only with the following third-party services, solely as necessary to operate the App:

  • OpenAI – speech-to-text transcription for the reading module
  • Supabase – database and authentication infrastructure
  • RevenueCat – subscription and in-app purchase management
  • Sentry – crash reporting and performance monitoring
  • Apple / Google – identity verification and payment processing

4. Data Retention

We retain your account and progress data for as long as your account is active. Audio recordings from the reading module are not retained on our servers — they are sent to OpenAI for transcription and the original audio is discarded once the transcription is returned. OpenAI may retain the audio for up to 30 days under its API data usage policy, after which it is automatically deleted. Transcription results and analytics derived from audio may be retained in aggregated form for service improvement purposes. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at privacy@notesurfing.com.

5. Children's Privacy

NoteSurfing is designed for use by individuals aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App may offer UX configurations that allow the registered adult user (13+) to adapt the learning experience for another learner, such as a younger student; in that case, the adult account holder is responsible for the interaction and for complying with their local regulations regarding children's use of digital services. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information directly, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@notesurfing.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, through in-app notification.
